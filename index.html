About me

Hi everyone, my name is Richard Depanfilis but I go by Anthony. I have been a photographer for two years now. I am based in Austin Texas. I was inspired to do this by the massive growth of content creation and social media. I am someone who believes in quality. I want to create and capture quality images and make content with them. So during the pandemic there was no better time to pick up on a hobby, I went and bought my first professional camera. Since then I have doubled my gear and began a social media page which has had substantial growth. I currently like shooting/filiming fitness, nature, street, lifestyle, and hope to get into proefessional portaits and even weddings. I love to travel when I can. I hope in the future I can make a lot of traveling inspired content. New York is one place I want to take a few trips to go and capture different spots in the city at different times of the year. I also love to collaborate and feed off of other photographers here in Austin.
